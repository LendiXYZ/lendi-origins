# Community Strategy — Lendi

## Target Audience

| Segment          | Who                                  | Where They Are         | What They Need                       |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Informal workers | Freelancers, gig workers in Colombia | Telegram, WhatsApp     | Prove income without exposing it     |
| Small lenders    | Microlenders, P2P lenders            | DeFi Twitter, Telegram | Protected capital deployment         |
| Credit circles   | Community lending groups             | WhatsApp, local        | Trust infrastructure for group loans |

## First 100 Users

Individual workers + small orgs on testnet in Colombia. Narrow pilot before large fintech outreach.

1. Personal network of informal workers in Colombia (10-20)
2. Small microlender partners willing to test (3-5 orgs)
3. Community credit circles in Bogota/Medellin (2-3 groups)
4. Fhenix/Reineira ecosystem builders at hackathons
5. Content-driven inbound from Spanish DeFi community
